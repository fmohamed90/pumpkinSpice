Pumpkin Spice Latte

The perfect drink for Fall

The Pumpkin Spice Latte is a coffee drink made with a mix of traditional fall spice flavors (cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove), steamed milk, espresso, and often sugar, topped with whipped cream and pumpkin pie spice. The beverage is most commonly associated with Starbucks, which first offered the drink in 2003. The popular flavor has inspired a wide range of product variations that appear on a seasonal basis.
